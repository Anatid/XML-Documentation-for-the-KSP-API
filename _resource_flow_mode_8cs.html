enum ResourceFlowMode

An enumeration of the different behaviors resources can have with respect to fuel flow.

Enumerator
NO_FLOW 

This resource cannot flow between parts (like SolidFuel).

ALL_VESSEL 

The flow scheme used by the stock ElectricCharge and IntakeAir resources.

EVEN_FLOW 

Unused?

STAGE_PRIORITY_FLOW 

The flow scheme used by the stock MonoPropellant and XenonGas resources. Resource can be draw from any container by any consumer, but it tries to prioritize drawing from continers in earlier stages.

STACK_PRIORITY_SEARCH 

This resource behaves like LiquidFuel or Oxidizer, and can only flow through crossfeed-enabled parts and fuel lines.
