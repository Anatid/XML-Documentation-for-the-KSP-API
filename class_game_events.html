EventData<GameEvents.FromToAction<Part, Part> > GameEvents.onPartCouple
static

Called on docking. Passes your event handler a FromToAction<Part, Part> ev. ev.from and ev.to are the two parts (docking nodes) that have been coupled together. Actually this event fires twice on docking, with ev.from and ev.to swapped the second time.
