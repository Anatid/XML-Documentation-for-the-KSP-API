enum AttachModes

Describes the two possibilities for how a part can be attached to its parent.

Enumerator
STACK 

The part is attached to its parent via a stack node; for example this is how vertically stacked fuel tanks are attached

SRF_ATTACH 

This part is attached to the surface of its parent; for example this is how radial decouplers are attached.
